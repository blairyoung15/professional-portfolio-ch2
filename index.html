About Me

Hello! My name is Blair Young, I'm a current full stack web developer student in the Washington University Bootcamp course. After many years of dabbling in basic web development, I decided to take a chance and dive in head first to become a full stack developer. After studying sport management, I started my professional career in professional sports, where I quickly picked up proficient adobe skills and got my start with web development. I started designing my own email templates for our communications to fans as well as developing personalized web pages for events at the area. Additionally, I started working behind the scenes on app development, customer relationship management (CRM) technology and data analytics.

I pivoted from professional sports to the family business a few years ago, which allowed for more opportunity to expand my development skillset. Through the course of a few years, I implemented a CRM system in the company and worked intensely on a business rebranding that helped bring the company to a modern look. After months of research and design, we launched the rebranding which included a brand new wensite with a greater user experience.

Throughout my experiences in the professional field, I found myself always wanting to understand more about development and coding. Once again, I decided to pivot and pursure my passion for web development.
